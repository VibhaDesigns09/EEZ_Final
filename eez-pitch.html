05 The Product

Three interventions.
All in the window banks cannot reach.

Before
Behavioral profiling
A short diagnostic maps each user's own digital risk pattern — which platforms they over-trust, where they tend to share, which scam archetypes match their behavior. People defend better against threats they recognize as theirs.
During
AI fraud detector
The user pastes any suspicious message — text, email, DM, voice transcript — and gets an instant read: safe, suspicious, scam. Trained on real fraud patterns. Fraud runs on speed; this is the pause that breaks it.
After
SOS mode
The moment someone realizes they've been hit, panic takes over. SOS walks them through the next 30 minutes — what to freeze, who to call, what to document — and pings their bank with a fraud-event flag.
